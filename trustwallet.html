HOW TO IMPORT TRUSTWALLET INTO METAMASK

To import your TrustWallet into MetaMask, you will need your TrustWallet Recovery Phrase. To find it, follow these steps:

1. Open the TrustWallet mobile app

2. Click on the wallet name at the top

3. Click on Show Recovery Phrase

4. Store the Recovery Phrase somewhere safe to be used in a further step below

If you do not already have MetaMask, follow these steps:

1. Install MetaMask

2. Open MetaMask and click on the Import Wallet button. Click on “I agree”

3. Enter your Recovery phrase from Trust Wallet into the input box

4. Set a complex password and click on the “Import” button

5. You have now imported your TrustWallet into your MetaMask account

If you already have MetaMask, follow these steps:

1. Open MetaMask. WARNING: PLEASE ENSURE THAT YOUR CURRENT METAMASK ACCOUNTS ARE BACKED UP BY CLICKING ON SETTINGS > SECURITY & PRIVACY > REVEAL SEED PHRASE. STORE THE SEED PHRASE IN A SAFE LOCATION. In the steps below you will import your TrustWallet which will overwrite your current accounts so you will need to re-import them with the Seed Phrase

2. Click on your MetaMask account’s profile picture and then click on the “Lock” button

3. Click on the “import using seed phrase” button at the bottom

4. Enter the seed phrase from TrustWallet and create a new password

5. Click on “Restore”

6. You have now imported your TrustWallet into your MetaMask account

To be able to see your NFT in your MetaMask wallet, switch to the xDai Network and import the token address.
